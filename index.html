Our Story

Where tradition meets modern artistry

Appetina was born from a deep love for Filipino food culture — the warmth of shared meals, the richness of regional flavors, and the joy of bringing people together. We reinterpret beloved classics with a contemporary lens, creating dishes that honor heritage while exciting modern palates.

Based in Iligan City, we serve clients across Mindanao, offering premium catering solutions for events of every scale — from intimate celebrations to large corporate gatherings.
